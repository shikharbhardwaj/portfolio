About Fin-Offshore



Fin-offshore provides financial consultancy and services to startups, technology companies, individual clients and multinational companies across the globe that do not have in-house financial expertise.

We supply our clients with high quality and cost effective products that include entire range of financial department services. By outsourcing the financial functions to us, you can focus on growing your business.

Our professionals provide complete systems and services that cover the entire financial sphere. We are committed to our customers and believe in long term relationship based on mutual cooperation.



We work on a simple principle under which companies with little or none self sufficiency in the financial sphere outsource their jobs to us in exchange of a promise of the best in the industry services. The tasks are handled as priority jobs and all the data is kept highly confidential and is completely safe with us
