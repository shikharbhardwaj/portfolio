About ISCO

The Olympiad is managed solely by Intellify, a not for profit organization in collaboration with Social Innovation Cell, NSS IIT Delhi with help of students of IIT Delhi. We have been able to come up with a unique set of methodologies that will enable us to measure what are the exact skills which a student needs to develop and what are his strengths and weaknesses and what can better than the competitive environment to measure your best potential!

The International Science & Creativity Olympiad is a platform for budding creators to show and enhance their creative talents and abilities. We all, at various stages of our life, have realized the lack such platforms which motivate us to think out of the box and not limit our thinking to rote learning. We want to motivate the new generation to think and conquer new heights!

The Olympiad will take them through a range of well organised systematic tests and events which will determine the various components that form the base of the innovative quotient of participants. The Olympiad will measure your CAIQ (Creativity and Innovation Quotient) through an offline test followed by an online test and then followed by an interactive event held at IIT Delhi in December.

The Olympiad simply wouldn’t end there, but it will provide a year-long mentorship to the participating students by connecting them with IIT students as and when required and will provide them regular tests and material to build their skills as per their weaknesses assessed in the Olympiad throughout the next year!
