Event guidelines

Rules, Procedure and Guidelines:

  • Students from Class 7 to 12 can participate. Medium of the test will be english.
  • There are three levels: Level 1 (Class 7 and 8 ) , Level 2 ( Class 9 and 10) and Level 3( Class 11 and 12).
  • Students can take part in a team of 4. However team with minimum 2 student is allowed though ideal team size would be 3.
  • Minimum number of teams for students from a school to participate is 5 across all standards (7th to­12th).
  • Round 1 would be held in the school of the participative student.
  • Round 2 may be an online olympiad which will also be given in the respective school.
  • Round 3 will be an interactive event where student teams will be required to perform various tasks and demonstrate their creativity.
  • Any school/student involving in any kind of unfair means while taking part in the olympiad would be barred and strict action would be taken against the student/ school.
  • Students can only register via their school.
  • All judgements taken by the admin would be final and binding. The admin is not liable for any kind of loss that may happen due to non­organization of the olympiad due to any act of god or unforeseen calamity or due to issue on side of participative school.
